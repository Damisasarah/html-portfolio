The Best Movie According To Sarah.

My top 3 movies of all times.

Vampire diaries.

I love this series so much, at first i thought i would not like it, but i gave it a shot and i ended up recomending this movie to every one i know. It is the best supernatural teen movie i have ever seen.

The originals.

This is another series i love so much, i am really a fan of seasonal movies, it makes me feel as tho i am following the characters life. The originals is actually a spin-off from vampire diaries.
It follows the life of a family, the michealson family. It indeed has a beautiful story line.

A tribe called Judah

This is a Nigerian single movie, it was written and directed by Funke Akindele. The movie follows the life of a single mother and her children and how they overcame the hurdled and troubles life throws at them. It is an inspiring movie.
